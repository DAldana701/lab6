Erosions

Visiting Rancho Palos Verdes brought amazing views, especially when it cames to the large and obvious marks of erosions near the beaches. When heavy rainfall seasons and the continuous land shifts, these natural occurances brings out the geological history of this location and although beautiful to see, it's unpredictable occurances can be dangerous and deadly.

Rocks

With heavy rainfall and land movement comes with erosions, and with erosions comes with large sedimentary rocks. These type of rocks stays at the surface of the Earth, sometimes even underwater, for a large period of time, accumlating minerals and slowly becoming sedimentary. They are scattered through out the beaches and while beautiful to see, its diverse sizes and shapes makes walking on the beaches quite difficult.
